Heavy benchmark surface

Long semantic application form

This benchmark is intentionally heavier than the baseline surface: more required fields, more repeated Yes / No groups, several selects, multiple required checkboxes, and a long stack of essay prompts.

The goal is to measure what happens when the benchmark looks closer to a real recruiting flow that spans multiple scroll depths and forces the automation path to keep payloads under control while still ending in a clean validation state.
